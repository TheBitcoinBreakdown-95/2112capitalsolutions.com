— 05 / Services (editorial rows)

This is the structural divergence from TBB. Services are editorial bands — full-width rows with a numeral, a generous title, prose lede, and a deliverables/price meta block. NOT a 3-card grid. One service per scroll-screen.

01 — Custody

Self-custody setup

Get your bitcoin off exchanges and into a hardware wallet you control. We handle device selection, seed generation, multisig setup, and a recovery plan you can actually trust. One-on-one, in person or remote.

  • Hardware wallet selection
  • Seed backup & recovery plan
  • Optional multisig configuration
  • One-on-one walkthrough
From $100 Single-session engagement
02 — Infrastructure

Node & Lightning

Run your own Bitcoin node and Lightning channels. We handle hardware procurement, full-node configuration, hardening, and ongoing maintenance — so your sovereignty stack stays current without becoming your second job.

  • Hardware procurement
  • Full-node + LN configuration
  • Security hardening
  • Optional monthly maintenance
From $500 Setup + optional retainer
03 — Advisory

Strategy consulting

One-on-one or business engagements. Treasury planning, integration strategy, education for teams. Hourly engagements; ongoing retainers for clients who need consistent counsel rather than a single project.

  • Bitcoin treasury planning
  • Workshop & team training
  • Custom integrations
  • Ongoing advisory retainer
$100 / hour Or retainer-based
