Each engagement is scoped against an explicit deliverable list. No discovery-call funnels. Pricing visible at intake. Retainer relationships available for ongoing infrastructure.

01.01 CUSTODY

Self-custody setup & key ceremony

End-to-end migration from exchange custody to self-held cold storage. Hardware procurement, seed generation under verified entropy, multisignature configuration where appropriate, recovery procedure with tested redundancy.

  • Hardware wallet selection + procurement
  • Seed ceremony with redundancy
  • 2-of-3 or 3-of-5 multisig configuration
  • Recovery rehearsal · signed · dated · documented

MODULE 02 — METHODOLOGYSEQ.001 → SEQ.005

How an engagement runs.

Documented sequence. Each step has explicit inputs, outputs, and a clean termination point if the engagement isn't a fit on either side.

SEQ.001INTAKE
Free 30-minute consult

Direct conversation about where you are, what you have, and what success looks like. No pitch. We tell you what we'd do; you decide whether we should.

SEQ.002SCOPING
Written engagement memo

Deliverables, timeline, price, and risks documented. You sign or don't. No expiring quotes, no pressure tactics.

SEQ.003EXECUTION
One operator on file

Same operator from start to finish. We don't subcontract custody work. The person you talked to in intake is the person doing the work.

SEQ.004HANDOVER
Documented runbook

Every engagement closes with a written runbook covering recovery, rotation, and ongoing maintenance procedures. The runbook is yours to keep.

SEQ.005RETAINER
Ongoing stewardship · optional

For node infrastructure and treasury work, a monthly retainer covers patching, rotation, and quarterly health checks. Cancellable on 30 days notice.
